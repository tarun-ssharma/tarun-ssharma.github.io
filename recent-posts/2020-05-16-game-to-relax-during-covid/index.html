Game to relax during the corona virus pandemic

Does the thought of going out scare you with all the pandemic news? What if you could have fun in some of the time that you are worried about COVID-19? Play this simple game I made using p5js. Your goal is to go out and collect virtual groceries whilst avoiding infected people!
